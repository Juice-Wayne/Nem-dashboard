NEM Rebids
=============

Real-time pre-dispatch change tracker for the NEM. Compares the latest two AEMO runs (5-min and 30-min) and shows what moved — prices, demand, interconnectors, and sensitivities.

Live: https://nem-rebids-beta.vercel.app/

How to use
----------
Open the link above. Switch between tabs (Prices, Demand, Interconnectors, Sensitivities, Actuals vs 5PD) to see different data. Use the region/interconnector dropdowns to filter. Data auto-refreshes every 30 seconds.

What to test
------------
- Do all tabs load with data?
- Does it auto-refresh when a new AEMO run comes through (~every 5 min)?
- Do the region/interconnector filters work?
- Are the biggest movers sorted to the top?
- Does copy-to-clipboard work for pasting into emails?
- Do the numbers look right compared to what you see in other tools?

Why Next.js over Power BI?
--------------------------
- Refresh speed: 30 seconds vs Power BI's 30-minute minimum scheduled refresh
- No infrastructure required: no Oracle drivers, no VPN, no on-prem gateway, no Power BI licence
- Shareable: just send a URL, works in any browser
